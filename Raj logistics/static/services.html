Door to Door(DTD)

Offers pickup of your shipment from your doorstep and
delivery at end point to the authorized representative on same day (24Hours).

Sensitivity to time same day pick-ups and same day delivery shipments
door-to-door (DtD) is performed with utter reliability and responsibility.

Currently, services for pick-up are active and
available from metro cities, viz. Bangalore, Chennai, Delhi,
and Kolkata across multiple locations.

Door to Airport (DTA)

Offers pickup of your shipment from your doorstep and
delivery at destination airport to your authorized representative.

We'll take into account any preferences you may have
because we want to make sure the entire
experience is enjoyable for you.

Airport to Door(ATD)

Offers pickup of your shipment from the nearest airport of your choice and
assured door delivery at your select destination across RAJ LOGISTICS network.

We are aware that your cargo is valuable and that you want to guarantee that
the appropriate materials are delivered to the appropriate location.
