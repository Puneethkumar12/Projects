Who are we?


Raj Logistics , Bangalore founded by a team of expert professionals from the logistics industry, is one of the leading startup logistics service providers specialized in air and surface cargo transportation through airport-to-airport and door-to-door deliveries covering destinations globally gearing to the needs of the 21st century. We take pride in serving our esteemed customers at all times. Our sophisticated technology-assisted-infrastructure supports web portal; online tracking, proof of delivery (PoD); and email communication services for business operations, business partners, and customer support creating information visibility to all stakeholders. We deliver business excellence in our logistics service performance through our supreme capabilities of careful cargo handling, communication clarity with stakeholders, and commitment to service excellence, providing customer delight at all times.
To enhance efficiency and provide effective logistic services to our trusting customers, our 3rd party service partners add power to our operations in rendering specialized services; such as SpiceXpress a division of Spicejet for airways mode and V- Xpress for surface transportation pan India; while, Total Courier & Cargo International Pvt. Ltd., Mumbai for global logistics services.
We respect your trust in us which is seamlessly acknowledged through our trustworthy service offerings with special regards to your time, money and care in reliable delivery of your cargo. Our unique selling proposition (USP) is same day deliveries (subject to airline connectivity) achieved through our leading service provider IndEx Aviation & Logistics networked with all airlines in India.
